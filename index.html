Welcome to POSTWEB Franchising

The POSTWEB was initiated by Alwyn Van De Merwe, who created MOCHACHOS chicken Franchise in 1993. POSTWEB is a one stop convenience franchise that partnered with DHL, Kodak, Courier guy, and Canon to deliver a range of efficient business solutions to the public and business sector.
POSTWEB strives to build a brand an quality, comprehensive product range beyond excellent customer service.
Our mission is to become the fastest successful growing franchise, Specialising in Printing, Courier, Stationery, Digital and Photo service industry

Our POSTWEB philosophy stems from recognition of our customers needs by practising these five cornerstones of a successful business

  • Quality Product
  • Best service
  • Value for money
  • Friendliness
  • Cleanliness

Our Franchisee's enjoy the benefit of a turnkey store store opening, Assistance with lease negotiations, Training in all aspect of the business and ongoing support. We keep our business model fresh and competitive in an ever changing business environment.

Prospective Franchisees should be :
  • A people person with an outgoing personality
  • A Team Player
  • Energetic and Passionate
  • A Promoter of family values
  • Ambitious
  • Quick an effective in decision making
